Executive SummaryThis document defines the system design for the Customer Success Operations module of Acme Corporation’s HubSpot CRM implementation. It specifies the custom properties, deal pipelines, and automation workflows required to operationalize Acme’s customer health monitoring and renewal management processes within HubSpot.The design is based on requirements gathered during three discovery workshops conducted between April 7–15, 2026, and has been validated through two design review sessions with Acme’s Customer Success and Revenue Operations teams.Upon approval, this document serves as the authoritative specification for all configuration work. Any deviations from this design require a formal change request and re-approval from both parties.2.
